Sidhu moose wala

Biography

Sidhu Moose Wala, born Shubhdeep Singh Sidhu, is a popular Punjabi singer and songwriter known for his unique blend of traditional folk and contemporary rap music. Hailing from Punjab, India, he rose to fame with hits like "So High" and became a prominent figure in the Punjabi music scene.
